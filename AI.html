Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a fairly new technology built with the intent to further humanities boundless creativity however when some people think of AI they cower in fear thinking of a robot revolution. Is this really happening or is it all just sci-fi mumbo-jumbo?






What AI is - it is fundamentally the ability for a computer to replicate human intelligence.





Benefits of AI

  • 1. Giving robots intelligence allows them to learn to do harder jobs which can aid in industry or the workforce improving efficiency


  • 2. Functions such as face and voice recognition use this and are convenient for people


  • 3. May allow higher technological advancements with the aid of this AI since they don't tire or forget information


  • 4. Can spot patterns and do certain tasks better than humans can








    • "With great power comes great responsibility - I have no idea"












    Drawbacks of AI

  • 1. Raises accountability questions, such as rights concerning sentient AI


  • 2. Could raise privacy issues in facial or voice recognition systems


  • 3. People may not be so accepting of AI since it has become synonymous with robotic revolution


  • 4. Could be dangerous if AI is created with ill intent
