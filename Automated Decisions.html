Automated Decisions

Automation is amazing and it's already everywhere, from social media feeds to the functions of commercial jets this concept is taking over but what does that mean for us? And how can this impact us in a way we can't initially see?









Benefits of Automated Decisions

  • 1. Usually allows ease of use to the consumer saving time on certain tasks which could take time or effort


  • 2. A computer based decision may be better than one made by a human and free of biases


  • 3. Applications are broad and so can be implemented anywhere


  • 4. Allows application processes to be more efficient and get to more people since people can be allowed to sleep








    • "There is no such thing as a free lunch - Milton Friedman"












    Drawbacks of Automated Decisions

  • 1. Having social media feeds automated may lead to a bubble forming, closing society off to certain ideas; possibly a form of censorship


  • 2. They could cause people to recieve unfair treatment if the code isn't written properly


  • 3. Could be dangerous in certain scenarios such as with driverless cars which may not give the passengers any control


  • 4. The automated decision may not be as indepth and calculated as a human may be able to produce


  • 5. Many legal issues can arise from any one of these with the company behind the program being held liable
