Computers In The Workforce

In recent times, more and more companies are resorting to using robotics and other technologies to replace and improve their workforce causing many people to lose jobs and subsequently their income and homes. However technology is also a big help in improving efficiency through better communication and lowering overall costs so what are our options?









Benefits of Tech

  • 1. Technology improves communication since employees can work together virtually via meetings and such while in different areas saving time travelling and allowing employees to work whenever they are available


  • 2. It gives you access to a wide array of information to help with research related tasks


  • 3. It makes it much easier to share documents since it's all digital and collaborate effectively


  • 4. Processes like printing and photocopying can be done quickly on site saving time


  • 5. It allows employees to feel more free at the workplace instead of restricted in options

    Drawbacks of Tech

  • 1. Communication and Messaging Breakdowns Due to the prevalence of computers in the workplace, email is now a common mode of professional communication


  • 2. Increased Employee Stress Learning new computer skills and programs can put stress on workers, especially older or less-educated workers


  • 3. Distractions and Interruptions be it notifications or messages


  • 4. Disconnectedness and Lowered Workplace Satisfaction due to the need to "stay connected"


  • 5. Pressure and Health Issues via cyber bullying or harrassment
